 Challenge Insurance and Claim Incident Analysis 

 📌 Key Findings
Claims are highest in certain states indicating regional risk concentration
Specific incident types (like collisions) generate higher claim volume
Fraud cases account for a noticeable % of claims, especially in:
High claim amount cases
Certain incident types
Customers with higher premiums tend to file larger claims
Higher deductibles slightly reduce claim frequency
📌 Recommendations
Focus fraud detection on high-risk states & incident types
Adjust premiums based on customer risk profiles
Introduce stricter checks for high-value claims
Encourage higher deductibles to reduce small claims
