Instagram Engagement Analysis 


✅ Insight 1
Reels show the highest engagement rate, indicating short-form video content performs best.

✅ Insight 2
Posts with moderate hashtags (5–10) perform better than too many hashtags.

✅ Insight 3
Long captions slightly improve engagement, but impact is not very strong.

✅ Insight 4
Follower growth does not always increase engagement — content quality matters more.

✅ Insight 5
Certain months show peak reach due to campaign or trending content.
